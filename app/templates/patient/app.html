Privacy Policy

Data We Collect: Personal information (name, age, contact), health records (vitals, prescriptions, lab results), and appointment history — solely for providing healthcare services.


How We Use It: To manage your medical records, communicate with your healthcare providers, process payments, and send appointment reminders.


Data Security: All data is encrypted at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authorised hospital staff only.


Your Rights: You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting the hospital administration.


Contact: For privacy concerns, contact the hospital at the number on your registration card.

Help & Support
📅 Booking an Appointment
Tap the + button or go to the Book tab. Select a doctor, choose a date and available time slot, then confirm.
💊 Medicine Reminders
Tap the heart icon in the top bar. Your active prescriptions are shown with timing and dosage instructions.
💳 Paying Bills Online
Go to Bills tab, find the pending invoice and tap "Pay Now". Complete payment via UPI, card, or net banking.
📥 Downloading Reports
Go to Records → Lab Reports. Tap the download button on any completed report to get the PDF.
📞 Hospital Helpdesk
For urgent assistance, use the Emergency Alert button on the home screen or call the hospital reception.
